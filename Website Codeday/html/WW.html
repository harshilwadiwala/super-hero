Wonder Woman is a superhero created by the American psychologist and writer William Moulton Marston (pen name: Charles Moulton),[3] and artist Harry G. Peter in 1941 for DC Comics. Marston's wife, Elizabeth, and their life partner, Olive Byrne,[4] are credited as being his inspiration for the character's appearance.[3][5][6][7][8] Wonder Woman appears in American comic books published by DC Comics.[9] The character is a founding member of the Justice League. The character first appeared in All Star Comics #8 published October 21, 1941[10] with her first feature in Sensation Comics #1 in January 1942. The Wonder Woman title has been published by DC Comics almost continuously ever since.[11] In her homeland, the island nation of Themyscira, her official title is Princess Diana of Themyscira.
